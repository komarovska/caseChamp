Advantages of living in Ukraine

New culture

Ukraine is a large and beautiful country with rich history and impressive historical background. By living in Ukraine, interational students get a chance to immerse into a totally new culture - original and unique, but at the same time modern and safe. Also, good infrastracture allows to travel Ukraine fast and comfortable, and to see lots of places.

Quality of education

Education in Ukraine nowadays is a mix of traditional academic approach, for which it has been known for decades, and the latest trends and innovations. Ukrainian IT-specialists are well-recognized and valued throughout the world.

European location

As Ukraine is located in the heart of Europe, traveling to other European countries is convenient and relatively cheap. Trips to Poland, Hungary or Germany during the vacation or just for the weekend has already become a nice habit of Ukrainian students. Other European countries are also easily accessable

Why programming is the ultimate choice

It's universal

No matter what country you are studying programming at - you will be able to apply your skills whenever you wish.

High salaries

In our rapidly changing world IT-specialists are valued and appreciated. Therefore, even their first salaries are much higher than average.

Comfrtable working conditions

IT-companies are famous for their employee appraisal programms and various bonuses for ther stuff.

You will never get bored

Programming is such a wide field of expertise that you will always be able to switch the direction of your job and to develop.

Lots of job offers

IT is one of a few spheres, in which labor demand exceeds labor supplly. There is always a wide choice of offers, both in companies and for freelance orders.

Fast growth

If you are a gifted programmer, your carrer path will be fast and steep, and you will not have to wait for decades till taking a leading position.
